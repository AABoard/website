Get Involved - Practitioners

Share your AI education experience and learn from the work of others.

Why Share Your Work?

As an educator, instructional designer, or education technology specialist, your hands-on experience with AI tools shapes the future of teaching and learning. AAB provides a platform to document and share your work in a structured, public-interest format.

How Practitioners Can Contribute

  • Submit case studies from your classroom or training programs
  • Share implementation details, challenges, and outcomes
  • Contribute replicability assets (lesson plans, rubrics, templates)
  • Participate in community dialogues with fellow practitioners
  • Provide feedback on draft research briefs and frameworks

Benefits for Practitioners

By contributing to AAB, practitioners gain:

  • Recognition for your work in a public, citable registry
  • Access to documented practices from peers across contexts
  • Connection to a growing community of AI education practitioners
  • Opportunities to shape emerging best practices and standards
  • Support in documenting and reflecting on your implementation

What We Document

Case submissions are documented with your input and review, covering:

  • Education level, subject area, and use case type
  • AI tools and implementation model
  • Governance and safety considerations
  • Evidence collected and observed outcomes
  • Constraints, risks, and lessons learned
