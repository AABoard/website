Get Involved - Researchers

Contribute to and utilize AAB's growing body of AI education research.

Why Work with AAB?

AAB provides researchers with access to structured, de-identified documentation of real-world AI education practices. Our case and pilot registries offer citable evidence for studying design patterns, adoption contexts, and emerging standards—without functioning as a human-subjects research dataset.

Research Opportunities

  • Access structured case documentation for literature reviews and meta-analysis
  • Submit your own research findings as case studies to the registry
  • Collaborate on research briefs analyzing trends across cases
  • Contribute methodological expertise to pilot design and evidence collection
  • Participate in cross-sector research dialogues with practitioners and policymakers

Using AAB Data

Researchers can cite case and pilot entries from the AAB registry in academic publications. Each entry includes:

  • Standardized metadata for filtering and comparison
  • De-identified implementation details
  • Evidence type and outcomes data where available
  • Replicability assets (playbooks, rubrics, templates)

Contributing Research

Have completed research in AI education? AAB can help document and share your findings in our public registry, increasing visibility and impact.
