Get Involved - Governments & Institutions

Partner with AAB to document and share AI education practices at scale.

Why Partner with AAB?

Governments, school districts, universities, and educational institutions play a crucial role in shaping AI education policy and practice. AAB provides a neutral, public-interest platform to document real-world implementations, share learnings, and contribute to emerging standards.

How Institutions Can Contribute

  • Submit case studies from your AI education initiatives
  • Participate in structured pilot programs with documentation support
  • Share de-identified implementation data to inform best practices
  • Collaborate on research briefs and evidence collection
  • Join community dialogues with peers across sectors

Partnership Benefits

By partnering with AAB, institutions gain access to:

  • Independent documentation and analysis of your AI education work
  • Cross-sector insights from the case and pilot registries
  • Recognition in the public registry with proper attribution
  • Participation in shaping emerging assessment frameworks
  • Connection to a growing network of education AI practitioners
